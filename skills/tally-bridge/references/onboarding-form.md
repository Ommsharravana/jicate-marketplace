## F. Data Protection & Privacy

> **DATA PROCESSING NOTICE**
>
> JICATE Solutions will access and process financial data from your TallyPrime installation as part of the Tally Bridge service. Please read the following carefully before providing consent.
>
> **Data types accessed:** Voucher records including sales, purchases, journal entries, payments, receipts, and contra entries.
>
> **Purpose:** Providing a real-time cloud dashboard for authorized business use by the client.
>
> **How data is processed:** Data is read from your TallyPrime installation via TallyPrime's XML API (Port 9000), transmitted via HTTPS to a secure cloud database, and displayed on a password-protected web dashboard.
>
> **Security:** Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).
>
> **Retention:** Your data is retained for the duration of the active service plus 30 days after termination. After this period, all data is permanently deleted from JICATE servers.
>
> **Access:** Restricted to authorized JICATE personnel and client-designated users only. No third parties will have access to your data.

**Your Rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023:**

- **Right to access** — You may request a copy of all data processed from your TallyPrime installation.
- **Right to correction** — You may request correction of any inaccurate data held by JICATE.
- **Right to erasure** — You may request deletion of your data. Deletion will be completed within 5 business days of receiving a written request.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — You may withdraw consent at any time. The Tally Bridge service will be discontinued upon withdrawal.
