# Campus Operations -- Rule-Based SDG Mapping

> These rules enable automatic SDG classification of campus operational data
> without requiring AI inference. Used for bulk mapping of facilities, utilities,
> transport, construction, and administrative metrics.

---

## Composite SDG Score Calculation Rules

### Per-SDG Evidence Aggregation

For each SDG, aggregate evidence from multiple campus operation categories:

**SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy):**
- Solar capacity (kWp) / total campus load (kW) x 100 = Renewable %
- LED fixtures / total fixtures x 100 = LED adoption %
- Current year kWh / previous year kWh = Energy trend
- BEE star-rated equipment count

**SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation):**
- Rainwater harvested (m3) / total water consumed (m3) x 100 = Harvest ratio
- STP treated water reused (m3) / total consumption (m3) x 100 = Reuse %
- Water consumption per student trend (liters/day year-over-year)

**SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production):**
- Waste recycled (kg) / total waste generated (kg) x 100 = Recycling rate
- Composting rate (% of wet waste composted)
- Paper consumption reduction year-over-year
- Green procurement spend / total procurement spend x 100

**SDG 13 (Climate Action):**
- Total Scope 1+2 emissions (tCO2e) / previous year = Emission trend
- Renewable energy % (from SDG 7)
- Carbon sequestration from green cover (estimated tCO2e)
- Climate education programs count

**SDG 15 (Life on Land):**
- Green area (m2) / total campus area (m2) x 100 = Green cover %
- Annual trees planted / existing tree count = Growth rate
- Biodiversity species count / baseline count = Diversity index
- Native species / total species x 100 = Native %

**SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities):**
- Green building certified area / total built-up area x 100
- Disabled-accessible buildings / total buildings x 100
- EV + bicycle mode share %
- Heritage/arts programs count

## Confidence Levels for Rule-Based Mapping

| Confidence | Description | Examples |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| HIGH (>90%) | Direct, unambiguous SDG mapping | Solar panels -> SDG 7, Rainwater harvesting -> SDG 6 |
| MEDIUM (70-90%) | Clear primary SDG, secondary debatable | Composting -> SDG 12 (primary), SDG 15 or SDG 2? |
| LOW (<70%) | Multiple valid SDG interpretations | Community adoption -> SDG 1 or SDG 11 or SDG 17? |

For LOW confidence mappings, the AI mapping engine should flag for human review rather than auto-assigning.

## Indian Regulatory Context

### NAAC Green Audit Requirements (Attribute 10)
All metrics above map directly to NAAC Attribute 10 evidence requirements.

### NIRF SDG Category
Campus operations data feeds directly into NIRF sustainability assessment.

### UGC Green Campus Guidelines (2023)
- Solar energy mandatory for new buildings
- Rainwater harvesting mandatory
- Waste segregation mandatory
- Green audit every 2 years

### AICTE Green Initiatives
- Mandatory Environment and Sustainability course (2023+)
- Green campus certification encouraged
- E-waste compliance mandatory
