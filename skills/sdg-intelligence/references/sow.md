## Scope of Work

### Included

1. AI-powered SDG mapping of institutional data (courses, research, outreach, campus operations, initiatives, policies) to all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals
2. SDG Scorecard generation with coverage percentages, evidence counts, and strength tiers across all 17 SDGs
3. NAAC Attributes 7, 9 & 10 report sections (new binary accreditation framework) — ready to paste into SSR document
4. NIRF SDG Rankings data preparation — evidence sheets matching the 2025 SDG evaluation parameters
5. THE Impact Rankings data preparation — per-SDG evidence sheets in THE indicator format
6. QS Sustainability data preparation — Environmental, Social, and Governance dimension evidence
7. Gap Analysis with prioritized recommendations for improving SDG coverage
8. Post-delivery support for the agreed support period (30 days for PaP, ongoing for MO)

### NOT Included

1. Actual NAAC/NIRF/THE/QS submission to the ranking body — client submits using our prepared data
2. Real-time dashboard or SaaS platform login — deliverables are reports (PDF + Markdown)
3. Student-facing SDG awareness modules or training
4. Workshop/training delivery (available as separate add-on — see quotation)
5. Environmental audit or carbon footprint calculation (different service)
6. Corporate ESG reporting — this service is for Higher Education Institutions only
