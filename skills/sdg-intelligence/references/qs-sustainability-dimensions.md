# QS Sustainability Rankings -- 3 Dimensions, 9 Lenses, 52 Indicators

> Source: QS Quacquarelli Symonds, World University Rankings: Sustainability
> Edition: 2025 (3rd edition). 1,744 institutions ranked.
> Structure: 3 Categories -> 9 Lenses -> 52 Indicators

---

## Overview

The QS World University Rankings: Sustainability evaluates the social and environmental impact of higher education institutions as centres of education and research, as well as assessing how well they navigate the challenge of operating sustainably.

Three categories are informed by nine lenses, measured by 52 indicators. Weightings are reviewed annually.

## Inclusion Criteria

To be included, an institution must:
1. Be eligible for QS World University Rankings, QS Regional Rankings, or QS Subject Rankings
2. Have a non-zero score in Environmental Research SDG metric (ER1)
3. Have a non-zero score in at least 2 of 4 Social Impact SDG research metrics (EO2, EQ1, HW1, IE1)

Business schools are not currently eligible.

## Additional Data Collected (Not Yet Weighted)

QS collects the following for future methodology evolution:
- **TASK by Sulitest** -- Sustainability knowledge assessment tool adoption
- **Energy consumption** -- Annual kWh consumed by institution
- **Scope 3 emissions** -- Indirect carbon emissions (tCO2e)
- **Student scholarship numbers** -- 50% and 100% fee coverage
- **Sustainability training** -- Mandatory annual training existence
- **Water consumption** -- Annual cubic meters consumed

## Key Differences: QS Sustainability vs THE Impact Rankings

| Dimension | QS Sustainability | THE Impact Rankings |
|-----------|------------------|---------------------|
| Structure | 3 categories, 9 lenses, 52 indicators | 17 individual SDG tables + overall |
| SDG coverage | Aggregated into themes | Per-SDG detailed indicators |
| Research data source | QS proprietary + Scopus | Elsevier |
| Survey data | Academic + Employer surveys | University-submitted evidence |
| Governance dimension | Yes (10%) | No separate dimension |
| Staff perception metrics | Yes (3 indicators) | No |
| Policy citations | Yes (3 indicators) | No |
| National statistics used | Yes (5 indicators) | No |
| Institutions ranked | 1,744 (2025) | 2,152+ (2025) |
| Year launched | 2022 | 2019 |
